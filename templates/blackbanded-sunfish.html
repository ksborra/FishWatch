Blackbanded Sunfish

Scientific Name: Enneacanthus chaetodon

Federal Status: Note Available
State Status: State Endangered

WAP Tier: I
WAP Rank: a

How to help!!

Conservation of populations of black-banded sunfish depends upon maintaining habitat quality in the Okefenokee Swamp and in other appropriate habitats. Habitat loss through pollution, drainage, and hydrologic alteration of Coastal Plain swamps, rivers, and wetlands must be avoided.
